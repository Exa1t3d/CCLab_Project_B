Curiosity

Created By :

Britt Burney, 04/23/2024

Description:

I created Curiosity to represent the insatiable desire of humanity to look beyond what lies before us and venture out into the unknown. The portentous treasure chest presented before our character captivating our protagonist, beckoning him to open it. After gazing into the mysterious treasure trove, he is sent throughout the space and time itself until he is transposed into a new realm. He now lies as a stranger amidst unfamiliar landscapes, his senses momentarily confounded by the tumultuous whirlwind of temporal displacement.
